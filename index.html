Timeline

  • January 2015

    All starts from a problem

    Everything started with a question. A problem occured to an acquaitance. He drifted during his dive because of the streams and lost the boat once arrived to surface. No way to see it, waves were too big."Can you imagine a system which would allows me to find the boat during my decompression steps ?"

  • June 2015

    Research Phase

    Disapointed by our respective 3rd year project'teams, we decided to get together and start this project as soon as possible. To make it accepted by the school we had to make researches and show that it was theorically possible to make it work. We found a method, made mathematical studies and submitted it to our professor.

  • September 2015

    Starting the Project

    With the help of a friend we started to work on the project in september. We ordered an Arduino Uno which was a good choice to start prototyping, a TFT Screen to display our GUI and all the devices we needed to make it work. Unfortunatly the Uno was not powerful enough to meet our requirements. We switched to and Arduino M0 Pro in December. Based on an Atmel 32Bits microcontroller, it's much more powerfull, complete and futureproof. Without any strong skills in microcontroller programming (nor 32Bits), we mainly learned to use it, set it up and use the Graphic Library. We completed our mathematical study and wrote the calculation code.

  • June 2016

    Autorization to continue in 5th year

    School projects are over in 5th year, but there is an option which substitute to other courses. We can conduct a Project by our own, with the subject of our choice. So we made a demand to continue our project in 5th year. It has been accepted by the school.
