Diving Compass

A boat tracker for Divers

The project started in September 2015. We had a 4th year to accomplish and a good idea. Long story short, the aim of the project is to allow the diver to quickly read the direction of the boat on a numeric compass. We didn't progressed so much the first year,we essentialy learned about 32bits micro-controllers. But now, with this first year and our respectives internships in embedded systems we are fastly achieving its different parts. You can read more about it on its dedicated website just below.

Marantz Troubleshooting

Analog electronic learning

I started to learn analog amplification during my second degree university thank to a passionate teacher. He designs his tube and transistor amplifiers himself. I bought an out of order Onkyo amp, and started to learn how it is functionning. Sadly I didn't solved the problem. It needed to change the main processor and I didn't had the tools to obtain and flash the firmware in it. I bought pioneer amplifiers and repaired something like 6 amps over 8.

Now I repair Marantz amps. The conception is by far better, the PCB design is much more documented. The price is not the same though. I already repaired like 15 amps/preamp and 5 FM Tuner. You can have a look at Marantz 4400, 170DC, 4220, 140, 3200. I am curently working on a Model Fifteen and a Marantz 250.
